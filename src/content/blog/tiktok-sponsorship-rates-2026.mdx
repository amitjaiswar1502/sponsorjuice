TikTok sponsorship rates in 2026 are primarily driven by average views per post, not follower count. Brands pay for reach, and a creator with 50,000 followers but 8,000 average views should price closer to their view metrics than their follower total.

> **TL;DR**: TikTok creators typically charge $0.005 to $0.012 per average view for dedicated sponsored videos, adjusted by niche and audience geography. A creator with 10,000 average views in the beauty niche targeting US audiences might charge $90–$216 per dedicated video.

## What is a TikTok brand deal rate?

A TikTok brand deal rate is the fee a creator charges a brand for sponsored content — dedicated videos, integrated mentions, Stories-style frames, or bio link placements. Unlike traditional CPM advertising, creator rates bundle production, audience trust, and distribution into one package price.

Industry data from <a href="https://influencermarketinghub.com/influencer-rates/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Influencer Marketing Hub</a> consistently shows that engagement and average views outweigh raw follower counts when brands allocate influencer budgets.

## TikTok rate benchmarks for 2026

| Creator tier | Avg views/post | Typical dedicated video rate |
|---|---|---|
| Nano (5K–20K views) | 5,000–20,000 | $25–$240 |
| Micro (20K–100K views) | 20,000–100,000 | $100–$1,200 |
| Mid-tier (100K–500K views) | 100,000–500,000 | $500–$6,000 |

These ranges assume a US/Canada-weighted audience and a standard beauty or lifestyle niche. Finance and tech creators often command 1.5–1.8× higher multipliers.

## How to calculate your TikTok sponsorship rate

Use this formula as a starting point:

**Rate = average views × platform coefficient × niche multiplier × geography weight**

SponsorJuice applies these coefficients:

- **Platform coefficient**: $0.005–$0.012 per view
- **Niche multipliers**: finance 1.8×, tech 1.5×, beauty 1.2×, comedy 0.8×
- **Geography weights**: US/Canada 1.5×, UK/Australia 1.3×, global 0.7×

### Example calculation

A tech creator with 25,000 average views and a US/Canada audience:

- Low: 25,000 × $0.005 × 1.5 × 1.5 = **$281**
- High: 25,000 × $0.012 × 1.5 × 1.5 = **$675**

## What deliverables should you include?

Brands respond better to packages than single-post quotes. Common TikTok deliverables:

1. **Dedicated 30–60 second video** — highest rate, full creative control for the brand message
2. **Integrated mention** — 60% of dedicated rate, woven into regular content
3. **Story frames** — 40% weight, good for limited-time offers
4. **Bio link placement** — 30% weight, valuable for conversion-focused campaigns

Bundle multiple deliverables together and price the package above a single-post rate to reflect combined reach.

## Why views matter more than followers on TikTok

TikTok's For You Page distribution means follower count does not guarantee views. A creator can have 200,000 followers but average 5,000 views if their content no longer reaches non-followers. Brands increasingly request screenshots of average view counts from the last 15–20 posts rather than follower totals.

When pitching, lead with average views, audience geography, and niche — not follower count alone.

## Negotiation tips for TikTok creators

- **Quote a range**, not a single number — ranges invite conversation
- **Ask about usage rights** — 90-day usage rights are standard; extended rights cost more
- **Price exclusivity separately** — blocking competitor brands for 30 days might add 20–50%
- **Don't accept the first offer** — brands expect negotiation on mid-tier deals

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much should I charge for a TikTok brand deal in 2026?

Most TikTok creators charge $0.005 to $0.012 per average view for a dedicated sponsored video, multiplied by niche and geography factors. A creator with 10,000 average views might charge $90–$216 in the beauty niche with a US audience.

### Do TikTok followers or views matter more for pricing?

Views matter more. Brands pay for actual reach, which is reflected in average views per post rather than total follower count.

### Should I charge less for TikTok than Instagram or YouTube?

Generally yes. TikTok coefficients are lower than Instagram ($0.007–$0.015/view) and YouTube ($0.015–$0.035/view) because of shorter content format and different production expectations.

### How do I pitch a TikTok sponsorship rate to a brand?

Send a short email with your average views, niche, audience location, and a rate range. Link a media kit and reference a recent post that matches the brand's category. Personalize one line about the brand's product or campaign.
