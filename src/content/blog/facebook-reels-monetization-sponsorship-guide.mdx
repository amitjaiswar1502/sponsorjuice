## 2. The Power of Meta Cross-Posting Bundles

Because Facebook and Instagram operate under Meta's ecosystem, creators can cross-post short-form vertical videos seamlessly between both platforms.

### How to Price a Meta Dual-Platform Bundle:
1. Calculate your [Instagram Reel rate](/calculator/instagram/).
2. Calculate your [Facebook Reel rate](/calculator/facebook/).
3. Combine both rates and offer a **15% Bundle Discount** to the brand.

**Example:**
- Instagram Reel (15,000 avg views): $200
- Facebook Reel (20,000 avg views): $180
- Standalone Combined: $380
- **Discounted Meta Package Price:** **$325**

Selling Meta bundles gives the brand total reach across two distinct audience segments while boosting your revenue per video produced.

## 3. Facebook Specific Sponsorship Guidelines

When publishing sponsored content on Facebook, follow platform compliance rules:

- **Branded Content Tag:** Always use Facebook’s native Branded Content Tool tag ("Paid Partnership with [Brand Name]") to satisfy Meta policies and FTC requirements.
- **Trackable Links:** Unlike Instagram feed posts, Facebook post captions support clickable hyperlinks. Include a custom UTM trackable link in the first two lines of your Reel caption.
- **Top Comment Pinning:** Pinning a brand’s landing page link in the top comment of your Facebook Reel can increase referral click-through rates by up to 40%.
