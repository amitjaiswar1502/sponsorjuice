One of the biggest misconceptions in the creator economy is that you need 100,000+ followers to earn meaningful income from brand deals. In reality, brands are spending a growing percentage of their influencer marketing budgets on **micro-creators** (5,000 to 50,000 followers).

Why? Micro-influencers frequently boast higher engagement rates, tighter niche focus, and more authentic trust with their audience compared to macro-celebrities.

> **TL;DR**: Micro-creators with 5k to 50k followers typically charge **$100 to $500 per TikTok/Reel** and **$300 to $1,200 per YouTube integration**. Pricing should always be based on average views and audience engagement, not just follower count.

---

## 1. Why Brands Love Working with Micro-Creators

Before setting your rates, it helps to understand what brands evaluate when reviewing micro-influencer pitches:

1. **High Conversion Rates:** Smaller audiences feel like close communities. Recommendations from niche creators convert at 3x–5x higher rates than generic celebrity ads.
2. **Cost Efficiency:** Brands can partner with 10 micro-creators for the price of one celebrity, diversifying risk across multiple creative angles.
3. **User-Generated Content (UGC) Potential:** Many brands hire micro-creators primarily to create high-performing ad creative for their own paid Meta and TikTok ads.

## 3. How to Calculate Your Micro-Creator Rate Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Find Your Real Baseline (Average Views)
Calculate the average view count across your last 10 organic posts (exclude viral outliers that have 10x your normal views). If your last 10 Reels average 8,000 views, use 8,000 as your benchmark.

### Step 2: Apply the Niche & CPM Formula
Multiply your average views by your niche CPM coefficient:
- **Consumer / Vlogs / Humor:** $8 – $12 CPM
- **Beauty / Fashion / Fitness:** $12 – $18 CPM
- **Tech / Software / Productivity:** $20 – $35 CPM
- **Finance / Business / Career:** $30 – $50 CPM

### Step 3: Factor in Add-ons & Usage Rights
If a brand asks for rights to run your video as a paid ad (Meta Spark Ads or TikTok Dark Posts), add a **30% to 50% monthly usage fee**.

## 4. Common Pricing Mistakes Micro-Creators Make

- **Accepting Free Product as Full Payment:** Gifting is fine when starting out, but once you consistently deliver over 2,000 views per post, always request a monetary sponsorship fee.
- **Underpricing Bundles:** Don't sell 3 Reels for the price of 2. Offer small bundle discounts (e.g., 10–15% off) to encourage multi-post campaigns.
